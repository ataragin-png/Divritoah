means: (1) upload a new PDF to files/ and (2) add a new entry at the TOP
of the FILES array in divrei-torah.html.

existing gold/cream design and CSS variables) with an "הכל" button as
the default that shows everything; clicking a category shows only its

Keep everything else on the page exactly as it is (RTL, design, header,
